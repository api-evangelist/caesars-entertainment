# Caesars Entertainment (caesars-entertainment)
Caesars Entertainment is the largest U.S. casino-entertainment company, operating over 50 gaming facilities, hotels, restaurants, and entertainment venues across the country and internationally. The company operates the Caesars Rewards loyalty program and offers gaming, hospitality, dining, entertainment, meetings and conventions, and retail experiences.
